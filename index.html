Mentorship

Since 2022, I have officially taken up some peer mentorship roles, with the objectives of

  1. Providing junior students with smooth transitions from previous secondary school academic systems to more comprehensive university exposures;
  2. Bridging the gradient of information asymmetry along seniority, consciousness, and quant industry knowledge;
  3. Upscaling information appropriately to derive greater benefits and impact.
Few people are kind enough in HKUST QFIN due to the inherent opportunism. I decided to be one and uphold my core values, despite the challenges and struggles I have faced: Quants rely on internal collaboration to survive external competition.
