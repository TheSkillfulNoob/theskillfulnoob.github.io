Reflections

Life can only be understood backwards; but it must be lived forwards. ― Søren Kierkegaard

My weekly reflection account was known as @sunmustsettorise online, established in January 2020.
Covering academics, philosophy, footprints and insights, such thought organization processes have now evolved into deep introspection sessions to navigate both personal and academic challenges effectively.

Motivations

The name "sunmustsettorise" comes from the lyrics of Coldplay - Paradise. I see my reflections carrying the following purposes:

Purifying Information

  • Sinks for my cluttered thoughts or emotions, by realizing them into words;
  • Containers to digest information in depth for sustained inspiration;
  • A media to speak out thoughts to, and seek greater audiences of interest.

Introspection and Retrospection

  • Records of frames of mind, extent of technological embracement and capabilities over time;
  • Exercises to clarify visions of who I want to be, and the purposes I wish to bring over time;
  • Footsteps, to enable utilizing these reflection passages later in life to not get lost.
The content density, writing styles and presentation of my weeklies vary over time. I assure you that some of them would be interesting to read :)

Year 4 - Digitalized Sun

I had a significant change in mentality. With content standardization into LaTeX presentations and proactive ChatGPT embracement in rationalizing my mind and content, weeklies this year mostly facilitated structured and mathematically heavy discussions on various quantitative concepts and quantifying their projections on greater societies.

  • Year 4 Passages

Year 3 - Solar Storm

Weeklies this year served as an internalizing, rationalizing mechanism to bring back my own pieces together after heavy traumas. I re-indexed my weeklies, increasing signposting and organization to improve readability.

  • Year 3 Passages

Year 2 - Violet Sun

As I tried to cultivate a flower in the middle of my dry emotional plains, my second-year weeklies featured struggles to strike balances between work obligations, theories and the intensity of feelings: love and pain.

  • Year 2 Passages

Year 1 - Rising Sun

My first university year weeklies focused on reflecting on my existence and listen to inner voice: With hindsight, it was a relatively carefree and joyful year as I find the new environment constructive.

  • Year 1 Passages

Birthday Weeklies

Near my birthday each year, I construct a summary of my feelings and experience over the year, featuring a themed philosophical discussion in each.
