Research

Qualitative Research

It is important to understand your surroundings to put capability to the fullest use. A brief catalogue of my past qualitative research included:

  • A private project, co-authoring a 300-page book on quant industry overview and relevant entry-level quant skillsets (2024 Summer);
  • Summaries on the finance industry 2023 overview (2023 Winter), and the collapse of FTX (2022 Fall);
  • Several report pieces in channelling academic information and industry insights exclusive to junior QFIN students (2022 - 2023).

Other Research Interests

Psychology enabled me to model people behaviours, identify individual patterns and optimize decision-making. My research is less formal, mainly in scattered population statistics on inter-personal behaviours.

  • Observing in the lens of well-established personality models such as OCEAN/ MBTI and Lumina Spark has improved my estimates on individual behaviours.
  • Learning systematic population patterns like Dunning-Kruger effect, Dunbar's friendship circle and Risk aversion extended my interpersonal vision.
  • I developed and investigated the concept of morality: Doing the right thing without being bound by legacy, ego exhaustion or loopholes in quantitative finance.
