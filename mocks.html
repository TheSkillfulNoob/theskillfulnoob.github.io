Mocks

Tutoring in MSC this semester has made me recognize the enormous demand of quality practice materials.

In my final week of undergraduate studies, I wish to be of help, by preparing a math mock paper set for Calculus I streams (MATH 1003/ 1012/ 1013).

  • Downloads (Released on 1 Dec)

Description and Rules

  1. The Calculus I papers contain 60-80% of (modified) past paper questions, and the rest, practical but advanced questions on your hot exam topics.
  2. Answers are NOT provided publicly. Only people with a hard copy and reference number on the release date (29/11/2024) may gain access to answers, free marking services and comments.
  3. Amendments to the paper were made on ALL streams. Please refer to the newest versions in downloads.
    • See Amendments

FAQs
